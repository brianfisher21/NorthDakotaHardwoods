Welcome to North Dakota Hardwoods

North Dakota Hardwoods was founded in 1985 as a hardwoods specialty shop but as population increase and demand raised we the need for a custom wood shop grew. By utilizing our expertise in hardwoods North Dakota Hardwoods became that very place. Now expanded into a 5,000 sq. ft. shop we specialize in hardwood flooring, top-notch cabinetry, high-end stairways and any other custom job that you throw our way while also keeping to our roots by offering unfinished exotic and domestic hardwoods for the do-it-yourselfer. As a full service stair building and cabinetry company, our experienced craftsman and artisans are ready to design and build your dream project.
